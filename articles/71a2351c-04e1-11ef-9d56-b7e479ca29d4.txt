                The Rampage missile is a long-range air-to-ground missile developed by Israel Aerospace Industries (IAI) and Israel Military Industries (IMI).

                The Rampage missile's combination of speed, range, and precision makes it a formidable asset in modern combat, significantly enhancing the operational capabilities 
                of the armed forces equipped with it.

            <p class="text-lg text-justify my-1">

                The missile leverages advanced navigation systems and a robust warhead to ensure maximum effectiveness against a wide range of high-value targets.
                Its supersonic speed and long-range capabilities allow it to be launched from a safe distance, reducing the risk to the launching platform and increasing survivability.

                enhancing its versatility in different combat scenarios.

              Operational Capabilities of the Rampage Missile

              providing a versatile and effective solution for modern warfare.

            <p class="text-lg text-justify">
              The Rampage's long-range and high-speed capabilities allow it to be launched from a safe distance, ensuring the safety of the launching platform. This is particularly important in modern combat scenarios
              where air defenses are increasingly sophisticated and capable of engaging targets at long ranges. The Rampage missile's ability to penetrate these defenses and deliver its payload accurately
              makes it a critical component of any modern air force's arsenal.
            </p>
